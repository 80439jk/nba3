Utility Assistance

Help paying electric, gas, and water bills in Clay County

LIHEAP – Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program

Federal program providing energy bill assistance and crisis intervention for eligible low-income Clay County residents. Covers electric, gas, and cooling costs.

📞 256-354-2245 🌐 Clay County DHR
Eligibility: Income at or below 150% of federal poverty level · Alabama ADECA administers LIHEAP statewide
Energy

Alabama Power / PowerShare Customer Assistance

Alabama Power offers budget billing, the PowerShare fund, and low-income discount rates for qualifying Clay County customers.

📞 1-800-245-4176 🌐 alabamapower.com/assistance
Electric

211 Alabama — Utility Crisis Referral

Call 211 anytime for immediate referrals to emergency utility assistance programs in Clay County, including after-hours crisis resources.

📞 Dial 211 🌐 211 Connect Alabama

Healthcare & Insurance

Alabama Medicaid, marketplace coverage, and free clinics

Alabama Medicaid provides free health insurance for qualifying low-income Clay County residents including children, pregnant women, seniors, and people with disabilities.

Apply: aces.alabama.gov
