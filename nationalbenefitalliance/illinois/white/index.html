Program

ComEd/Ameren CARE Program

Utility company customer assistance program offering bill payment plans, low-income discounts, and budget billing for qualifying Illinois residents with ComEd or Ameren service.

Housing & Rental Assistance

3 programs in Illinois

Illinois Rental Payment Program (ILRPP)

Emergency rental assistance for Illinois residents facing eviction due to financial hardship. Provides direct payment to landlords for past due and future rent.

📞 1-866-454-3571
Program

Section 8 Housing Choice Voucher

Federally-funded rental assistance vouchers for income-qualifying households in Illinois. Apply through your local public housing authority when waitlists open.

📞 1-866-454-3571
Program

IHDA Homeowner Assistance

Illinois Housing Development Authority provides mortgage assistance for homeowners facing financial hardship. Covers mortgage payments, property taxes, insurance, and HOA fees.

📞 1-877-456-2656
Program

Healthcare & Medicaid

3 programs in Illinois

Free or low-cost health insurance for qualifying Illinois residents including families, pregnant women, seniors, children, and people with disabilities.
