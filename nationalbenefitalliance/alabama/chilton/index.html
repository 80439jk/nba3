Utility Assistance

Help paying electric, gas, and water bills in Chilton County

LIHEAP – Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program

Federal program providing energy bill assistance and crisis intervention for eligible low-income Chilton County residents. Covers electric, gas, and cooling costs.

📞 205-755-2573 🌐 Chilton County Community Action
Eligibility: Income at or below 150% of federal poverty level · Alabama ADECA administers LIHEAP statewide
