FAQ

Stone County Benefits FAQ

How do I apply for SNAP benefits in Stone County?

Apply for SNAP through Arkansas DHS at ACCESS Arkansas (https://access.arkansas.gov) or call your local DHS office. You can also visit the Stone County Department of Human Services. Bring proof of income, ID, and residency.

Who do I call for emergency utility assistance in Stone County?

Contact your local community action agency for LIHEAP energy assistance. You can also call 211 anytime for referrals to emergency utility assistance programs available in Stone County.

Is there emergency rental assistance in Stone County?

Contact your local DHS office in Stone County or call 211 for current rental assistance availability. Programs open and close based on funding — call first to check status.
