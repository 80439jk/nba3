Statewide California Programs

⚡ LIHEAP & REACH

Energy assistance for low-income households. Apply through your county or call 211.

🍎 CalFresh (SNAP)

Monthly grocery benefits for individuals and families. Apply at BenefitsCal.com.

🏥 Medi-Cal

Free or low-cost health coverage for California residents. Apply at CoveredCA.com.

🏠 Housing Assistance

Section 8 vouchers, emergency rental help, and eviction prevention through county housing authorities.

💼 Unemployment (EDD)

File for unemployment benefits at edd.ca.gov or call 800-300-5616.

👶 CalWORKs

Cash assistance and services for low-income families with children. Apply at your county social services office.
