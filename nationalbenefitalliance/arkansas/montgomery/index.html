Utility Assistance

Help paying electric, gas, and water bills in Montgomery County

LIHEAP – Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program

Federal program providing energy bill assistance and crisis intervention for eligible low-income Montgomery County residents. Covers electric, heating, and cooling costs.

Eligibility: Income at or below 150% of federal poverty level · Managed by: Arkansas DHS

FAQ

Montgomery County Benefits FAQ

How do I apply for SNAP in Montgomery County?

Apply online at humanservices.arkansas.gov, call 1-855-355-3292, or visit your local DHS office. Bring proof of income, ID, and residency. Processing takes about 30 days, or 7 days if you qualify for expedited approval.

How do I get emergency utility help in Montgomery County?

Contact Arkansas DHS at 1-855-355-3292 for LIHEAP (Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program). For 24/7 emergency referrals, call 211.

How do I enroll in Arkansas Medicaid?

Apply through Access Arkansas at https://access.arkansas.gov or call 1-855-355-3292. You may also visit your local DHS office for in-person assistance. Eligibility is based on income and household size.
