Utility Assistance

Help paying electric, gas, and water bills in Kern County

LIHEAP – Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program

Federal program providing energy bill assistance and crisis intervention for eligible low-income Kern County residents. Covers electric, gas, and cooling costs.

📞 661-868-1000 🌐 Kern County Department of Human Services
Eligibility: Income at or below 150% of federal poverty level · Contact: 661-868-1000
Energy

CARE / FERA – California Alternate Rate for Energy

California utility discount programs offering 20-30% off monthly electric and gas bills for qualifying low-income Kern County households.

📞 1-800-627-1111 🌐 cpuc.ca.gov/low-income
Electric

211 – 211 Kern County Utility Crisis Referral

Call 211 anytime for immediate referrals to emergency utility assistance programs in Kern County, including after-hours crisis resources.

📞 Dial 211 🌐 www.capk.org/211
Referral

Housing & Rental Assistance

Rent help, eviction prevention, and housing stability programs

Housing Authority of Kern County – Emergency Rental Assistance

Emergency rental and mortgage assistance for Kern County residents facing eviction or foreclosure due to financial hardship.

📞 661-631-8500 🌐 www.kernhousing.com
Rental Aid

Section 8 Housing Choice Voucher Program

Federally-funded rental assistance vouchers for income-qualifying households in Kern County. Apply through your local housing authority when waitlists open.

Section 8

California Homeowner Assistance Fund (HAF)

Mortgage assistance for homeowners in Kern County facing financial hardship. Covers mortgage payments, property taxes, insurance, and HOA fees.

Food Assistance

CalFresh, food banks, WIC, and free meal programs

CalFresh – California Food Assistance (SNAP)

Apply for monthly food benefits through BenefitsCal. CalFresh helps Kern County residents buy groceries with an EBT card. Average benefit: $200+/month per person.

📞 1-877-847-3663 🌐 benefitscal.com
Apply online at BenefitsCal.com or visit Kern County Department of Human Services in Bakersfield · Processing: 30 days (3 days expedited)
CalFresh

Community Action Partnership Kern

Local food bank serving Kern County residents. Find the nearest food pantry, mobile distribution, or partner agency near you.

📞 661-336-5236 🌐 www.capk.org
Food Bank

WIC – Women, Infants & Children Program

Monthly food benefits, nutrition counseling, and breastfeeding support for pregnant women, new mothers, and children under age 5 in Kern County.

📞 1-800-843-2637 🌐 cdph.ca.gov/wic
WIC

Healthcare & Medi-Cal

Free and low-cost healthcare, Medi-Cal enrollment, and clinics

Medi-Cal – California Medicaid Health Coverage

Free or low-cost health insurance for qualifying Kern County residents including families, pregnant women, seniors, and people with disabilities.

📞 1-800-541-5555 🌐 benefitscal.com

Covered California – Health Insurance Marketplace

Shop for low-cost health insurance plans during open enrollment. Kern County residents may qualify for significant subsidies to reduce monthly premiums.

📞 1-800-300-1506 🌐 coveredca.com
Insurance

Federally Qualified Health Centers (FQHCs)

Community health centers in Kern County offer primary care, dental, and mental health services on a sliding-fee scale regardless of insurance status.

📞 1-800-548-4433 🌐 findahealthcenter.hrsa.gov
