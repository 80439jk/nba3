Utility Assistance

Help paying electric, gas, and water bills in Tillman

LIHEAP — Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program

Federal program providing energy bill assistance and crisis intervention for eligible low-income Tillman residents. Covers electric, gas, propane, and cooling costs. Administered by Oklahoma Department of Human Services (OKDHS).

📞 1-800-4-ENERGY 🌐 LIHEAP National Hotline
Application Period 🗓️ Seasonal
Oct 1 – Aug 15 (or until funds exhausted)
Priority: Seniors 60+, disabled, families w/ young children, disconnected utilities.
Eligibility Household income ≤ 60% of State Median Income
1 person: ~$2,600/mo · 4 people: ~$5,000/mo
Documents Needed
  • Proof of income (last 30 days)
  • Recent utility bill
  • Social Security numbers for household
  • Photo ID for head of household
How to Apply
📞 State LIHEAP Office 📞 Dial 211 🏢 Local CAA Office

Utility Company Low-Income Program

Oklahoma's major utility companies offer reduced rates, bill payment plans, and hardship programs for qualifying low-income customers. Separate from LIHEAP — you can use both.

📞 Check Your Bill 🌐 Find Your Utility
Application Period 🔄 Rolling
Apply anytime. No seasonal deadline.
Eligibility Must be a residential customer. Income ≤ 200% FPL or enrolled in SNAP/Medicaid/LIHEAP.
How to Apply

Housing & Rental Assistance

Rent help, eviction prevention, and housing stability programs

Emergency Rental Assistance Program

Emergency rental assistance for Tillman residents facing eviction. Provides rental payment coverage for past-due and future rent. Contact your local housing authority or social services office.

📞 Call 211 🌐 Find Local ERA Programs
Application Period ✅ Currently Available
Availability varies by county. Call 211 to check if programs are open.
Eligibility Facing eviction or housing instability. Income requirements vary. Most programs prioritize tenants with low/no income.
Documents Needed
  • Proof of income (or lack thereof)
  • Lease or rental agreement
  • Proof of housing instability (eviction notice, late notice)
How to Apply
📞 Call 211 🌐 Find Program
Rental Aid

Section 8 Housing Choice Vouchers

Federally-funded rental subsidy vouchers covering the difference between 30% of your income and fair market rent. Administered locally by your county housing authority.

📞 Call 211 🌐 HUD Section 8 Info
Waitlist Status 🔒 Often Closed
Many areas have long waitlists that are frequently closed. Check your local housing authority website for current status.
Eligibility Household income ≤ 50% of Area Median Income (AMI)
1 person: ~$30,000–35,000/yr
4 people: ~$45,000–50,000/yr (varies)
How to Apply Contact your local county/city housing authority to see if they're accepting applications or maintaining a waitlist. Even if closed, sign up to be notified when reopened.

Food Assistance

SNAP, food pantries, and nutrition programs in Tillman

SNAP (Food Stamps)

SNAP (Food Assistance) providing monthly EBT food benefits for low-income Tillman residents. Purchase food at authorized retailers. Faster processing through online applications.

📞 1-800-SNAP (7627) 🌐 Your State SNAP Portal
Application Period 🔄 Rolling
Apply anytime. Standard: 30 days. Expedited: 5 days if eligible.
Eligibility Gross income ≤ 130–165% FPL (varies by state)
1 person: $1,500–1,700/mo
4 people: $3,100–3,500/mo
No asset test in most states.
Documents Needed
  • Photo ID
  • Social Security numbers
  • Proof of income (pay stubs, SSA letter)
  • Proof of housing costs (rent/utilities)
How to Apply
🌐 Online (Fastest) 📞 Phone 🏢 Local Office

WIC — Women, Infants & Children Nutrition Program

Supplemental nutrition for pregnant women, new mothers, infants, and children up to age 5. Provides vouchers for milk, eggs, fruits/vegetables, cereal, and infant formula, plus nutrition education and healthcare referrals.

📞 1-800-WIC-BREASTFEEDING 🌐 WIC Program Info
Who Qualifies 🔄 Rolling
Pregnant, breastfeeding, or postpartum women; infants and children ≤ 5. Income ≤ 185% FPL. Auto-eligible if on Medicaid/SNAP.
Documents Needed
  • ID for parent/guardian
  • Proof of income or benefit card
  • Proof of residency (utility bill, lease)
  • Proof of pregnancy or child's birth certificate

Employment & Unemployment

Unemployment insurance, job training, and workforce development in Tillman

Unemployment Insurance — Oklahoma

Oklahoma unemployment benefits for Tillman workers who lost their job through no fault of their own. Weekly payments for up to 26 weeks. File online, by phone, or in person.

📞 1-800-UNEMPLOY 🌐 State Unemployment Office
Eligibility Lost job through no fault of your own. Earned minimum amount in base year. Week wait. Check your state for exact requirements.
Benefits (varies by state) Weekly payment: $50–$800+ depending on prior earnings
Duration: up to 26 weeks
Documents Needed
  • Social Security number
  • Driver's license or ID
  • Employment history
  • Reason for separation from job
How to Apply
🌐 Online (Fastest) 📞 Phone 🏢 Local Office
