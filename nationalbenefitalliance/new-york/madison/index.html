Housing & Rental Assistance

3 programs in New York

NY Emergency Rental Assistance Program (ERAP)

Emergency rental and utility assistance for Madison County renters facing eviction or housing instability due to pandemic-related financial hardship. Coverage includes back rent and future payments.

📞 1-844-691-7368
Program

Section 8 Housing Choice Voucher Program

Federally-funded rental assistance vouchers for income-qualifying households in Madison County. Subsidizes up to 70% of rent for eligible families and individuals.

📞 1-800-342-3009
Program

NY Homeowner Assistance Fund (HAF)

Mortgage assistance for Madison County homeowners facing financial hardship. Covers mortgage payments, property taxes, insurance, HOA fees, and utilities.

📞 1-844-776-9423

Food Assistance

3 programs in New York

SNAP via myBenefits.ny.gov

Apply for monthly food assistance (SNAP, formerly food stamps). Average New York SNAP benefit: $280/month per person. Apply online in minutes or at your local DSS office.

📞 1-800-342-3009
Program

Food Bank of Central New York

Regional food bank serving Madison County. Find the nearest food pantry, mobile distribution site, and partner agencies in your community. Emergency food assistance available.

📞 315-492-7916
Program

WIC – Women, Infants & Children Program

Monthly food benefits, nutrition counseling, and breastfeeding support for pregnant women, new mothers, and children under age 5 in Madison County.

📞 1-800-522-6006
Program

3 programs in New York

NY Medicaid via NY State of Health

Free or low-cost health insurance for qualifying Madison County residents including families, pregnant women, seniors, and people with disabilities.

Program

Federally Qualified Health Centers (FQHC)

Community health centers in Madison County provide primary care, dental, mental health, and other services on a sliding-fee scale regardless of insurance status.

Program

Child Health Plus

Low-cost health coverage for uninsured children in Madison County who don't qualify for Medicaid. Covers doctor visits, prescriptions, dental, vision, and mental health.
