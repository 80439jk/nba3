Our Mission

Built for People Facing Real Hardship

We started National Benefit Alliance after seeing firsthand how difficult it is for families in crisis to find the help they need. Government assistance programs exist across every state and county — but they're scattered across hundreds of websites, buried in bureaucracy, and often nearly impossible to find when you're under pressure.

Our team built this platform to change that. We gather, organize, and simplify access to benefit programs so that anyone — regardless of their tech skills, language, or situation — can find help in minutes, not weeks.

We believe that a parent choosing between paying the electric bill and buying groceries shouldn't also have to spend hours searching for assistance. That's why everything we do is free, always.

Whether you're a single parent navigating job loss, a senior on a fixed income, a veteran transitioning to civilian life, or anyone experiencing unexpected hardship — this resource was built for you.

No one should have to navigate hardship alone. When life gets hard, the path to help should be simple.

— National Benefit Alliance Founding Principle

How It Works

Two Ways to Get Help

Use our free online resource center to find programs on your own, or call our team for personalized guidance — whatever works best for you.

1

Search Online — Free & Instant

Enter your ZIP code on our homepage and we'll show you a personalized list of government and community assistance programs available in your specific county. Browse by category — utilities, housing, food, healthcare, and more.

2

Review Your County Resources

Each county page includes program details, eligibility requirements, contact information, office locations, and direct links to apply. Everything is organized clearly so you can take action right away.

3

Call for Personalized Help

Need extra support? Our benefits specialists can help identify programs you qualify for, explain eligibility, and guide you through the application process — all over the phone.

1-888-408-5650
4

Get Enrolled & Get Relief

With the right programs identified, you'll have clear next steps to apply and get enrolled. Many programs offer fast-track approvals for emergency situations — our team can help expedite the process.
