Utility Assistance

Help paying electric, gas, and water bills in Miami-Dade County

LIHEAP – Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program

Federal program providing energy bill assistance and crisis intervention for eligible low-income Miami-Dade residents. Covers electric, gas, and cooling costs.

📞 (305) 576-5001 🌐 communityactionmiamidade.org
Eligibility: Income at or below 150% of federal poverty level · Address: 2601 NW 5th Ave, Miami, FL 33127
Energy

FPL – Florida Power & Light Customer Assistance

FPL's Energy Assistance Program offers bill payment plans, low-income discounts, and budget billing for qualifying customers in Miami-Dade.

📞 1-800-375-4357 🌐 fpl.com/assistance
Electric

Miami-Dade Water & Sewer – Low Income Discount

Miami-Dade County WASD offers a 25% discount on water and sewer charges for income-qualifying households.

📞 (305) 596-6000 🌐 miamidade.gov/water
Water

211 Miami — Utility Crisis Referral

Call 211 anytime for immediate referrals to emergency utility assistance programs across Miami-Dade, including after-hours crisis resources.

📞 Dial 211 🌐 211miami.org

FAQ

Miami-Dade County Benefits FAQ

How do I apply for SNAP in Miami-Dade?

Apply online at myflorida.com/accessflorida or call 1-866-762-2237. You can also visit any DCF ACCESS Service Center in Miami-Dade. Bring proof of income, ID, and residency.

Who do I call for emergency utility help?

Call Community Action Agency of Miami-Dade at (305) 576-5001 for LIHEAP. For 24/7 referrals, call 211.

Is there rental assistance available right now?

Contact Miami-Dade Community Action at (305) 375-4680 for current rental assistance availability. Programs open and close based on funding — call first to check status.
