FAQ

Dimmit County Benefits FAQ

How do I apply for SNAP in Dimmit County?

Apply for SNAP at yourtexasbenefits.com or call 2-1-1. You can also visit any HHSC office in Dimmit County. Bring proof of income, ID, and residency.

Who do I call for emergency utility help?

Call 211 Texas for LIHEAP referrals. Local Community Action Agency at (713) 667-9400 also provides emergency utility assistance.

Is there rental assistance available right now?

Contact Dimmit County Community Services at (832) 927-4500 for current program availability. Programs open and close based on funding — call first to check status.
