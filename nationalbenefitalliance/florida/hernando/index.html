Utility Assistance

Help paying electric, gas, and water bills in Hernando County

LIHEAP – Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program

Federal program providing energy bill assistance and crisis intervention for eligible low-income Hernando County residents. Covers electric, gas, and cooling costs.

📞 352-754-7001 🌐 Hernando County Human Services
Eligibility: Income at or below 150% of federal poverty level · Contact: (352) 754-4001
Energy

FPL / Duke Energy – Utility Customer Assistance

Florida utility companies offer bill payment plans, low-income discounts, and budget billing for qualifying customers in Hernando County.

📞 1-800-375-4357 🌐 fpl.com/assistance
Electric

211 Tampa Bay — Utility Crisis Referral

Call 211 anytime for immediate referrals to emergency utility assistance programs in Hernando County, including after-hours crisis resources.

📞 Dial 211 🌐 www.211tampabay.org
Referral

Housing & Rental Assistance

Rent help, eviction prevention, and housing stability programs

Emergency rental and mortgage assistance for Hernando County residents facing eviction or foreclosure due to financial hardship.

Mortgage assistance for homeowners in Hernando County facing financial hardship. Covers mortgage payments, property taxes, insurance, and HOA fees.

Food Assistance

SNAP, food banks, WIC, and free meal programs

SNAP — Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (Food Stamps)

Apply for monthly food assistance through ACCESS Florida. Average Florida SNAP benefit: $210/month per person.

📞 1-866-762-2237 🌐 myflorida.com/accessflorida
Apply online or visit a DCF ACCESS Service Center in Brooksville · Processing: 30 days (7 days expedited)
SNAP

Neighborhood Health Foundation / Spring Hill Food Pantry

Regional food bank serving Hernando County. Find the nearest food pantry, mobile distribution, or partner agency near you.

📞 (352) 754-7001 🌐 www.feedtampa.org
Food Bank

WIC — Women, Infants & Children Program

Monthly food benefits, nutrition counseling, and breastfeeding support for pregnant women, new mothers, and children under age 5 in Hernando County.

📞 1-800-447-2229 🌐 floridahealth.gov/wic

Healthcare & Medicaid

Free and low-cost healthcare, Medicaid enrollment, and clinics

Florida Medicaid — DCF Health Coverage

Free or low-cost health insurance for qualifying Hernando County residents including families, pregnant women, seniors, and people with disabilities.
