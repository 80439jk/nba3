Frequently Asked Questions

How do I apply for SNAP in Clark County?

Apply online at humanservices.arkansas.gov or call 1-800-997-0772. You can also visit your local DHS office in Arkadelphia. Processing typically takes 30 days, or 7 days for expedited benefits.

What's the fastest way to get utility assistance?

Call 211 Arkansas anytime for an immediate referral to emergency utility assistance. For LIHEAP, contact Arkansas Community Action Partnership at 1-800-331-1126.

Is there Medicaid in Clark County?

Yes. Apply for Arkansas Medicaid online at access.arkansas.gov or humanservices.arkansas.gov, or call 1-844-241-2774. Coverage is available for families, seniors, children, and people with disabilities.
