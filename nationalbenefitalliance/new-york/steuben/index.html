Utility Assistance

Help paying electric, gas, and water bills in Steuben County

HEAP – Home Energy Assistance Program

Federal program providing energy bill assistance and crisis intervention for eligible low-income Steuben County residents. Covers electric, gas, and heating costs during winter months.

📞 1-800-342-3009 🌐 otda.ny.gov/programs/heap
Eligibility: Income at or below 60% of state median · Applications accepted September-June
Energy

Utility Company Assistance Programs

Steuben County utility companies offer bill payment plans, low-income discounts, and budget billing for qualifying customers. Contact your local utility provider for availability.

📞 Dial 211 NY 🌐 211nys.org
Electric

211 NY – Utility Crisis Referral

Call 211 anytime for immediate referrals to emergency utility assistance programs in Steuben County, including after-hours crisis resources and local provider information.

📞 Dial 211 🌐 211nys.org
Referral

Housing & Rental Assistance

Rent help, eviction prevention, and housing stability programs

NY Emergency Rental Assistance Program (ERAP)

Emergency rental and utility assistance for Steuben County renters facing eviction or housing instability due to pandemic-related financial hardship. Coverage includes back rent and future payments.

📞 1-844-691-7368 🌐 otda.ny.gov/emergency-rental-assistance
Income limit: 80% of area median · Covers up to 15 months back and future rent
Rental Aid

Section 8 Housing Choice Voucher Program

Federally-funded rental assistance vouchers for income-qualifying households in Steuben County. Subsidizes up to 70% of rent for eligible families and individuals.

📞 1-800-342-3009 🌐 hcr.ny.gov/public-housing
Wait times: Apply through your county housing authority · Long waitlists expected
Section 8

NY Homeowner Assistance Fund (HAF)

Mortgage assistance for Steuben County homeowners facing financial hardship. Covers mortgage payments, property taxes, insurance, HOA fees, and utilities.

📞 1-844-776-9423 🌐 nyhomeownerfund.org
Eligibility: Income at or below 100% of area median · Up to $50,000 in assistance
Mortgage
🥗

Food Assistance

SNAP, food banks, WIC, and free meal programs

SNAP via myBenefits.ny.gov

Apply for monthly food assistance (SNAP, formerly food stamps). Average New York SNAP benefit: $280/month per person. Apply online in minutes or at your local DSS office.

📞 1-800-342-3009 🌐 mybenefits.ny.gov
Processing: 30 days standard, 7 days expedited · Works at most grocery stores
SNAP

Regional Food Bank of Northeastern NY

Regional food bank serving Steuben County. Find the nearest food pantry, mobile distribution site, and partner agencies in your community. Emergency food assistance available.

📞 518-434-1917 🌐 Call 211 to locate nearby pantries
Food Bank

WIC – Women, Infants & Children Program

Monthly food benefits, nutrition counseling, and breastfeeding support for pregnant women, new mothers, and children under age 5 in Steuben County.

📞 1-800-522-6006 🌐 health.ny.gov/wic
Income limit: Up to 185% of federal poverty level
WIC
🏥

Healthcare & Medicaid

Free and low-cost healthcare, Medicaid enrollment, and clinics

NY Medicaid via NY State of Health

Free or low-cost health insurance for qualifying Steuben County residents including families, pregnant women, seniors, and people with disabilities.

📞 1-855-355-5777 🌐 nystateofhealth.ny.gov
Income limit: Up to 150% of federal poverty level
Medicaid

Federally Qualified Health Centers (FQHC)

Community health centers in Steuben County provide primary care, dental, mental health, and other services on a sliding-fee scale regardless of insurance status.

📞 1-800-548-4433 🌐 findahealthcenter.hrsa.gov
Health Clinic

Child Health Plus

Low-cost health coverage for uninsured children in Steuben County who don't qualify for Medicaid. Covers doctor visits, prescriptions, dental, vision, and mental health.

📞 1-800-698-4543 🌐 health.ny.gov/child-health-plus
Children

Employment & Unemployment

Unemployment benefits, job training, and workforce programs

NY Department of Labor Unemployment Insurance

Apply for weekly unemployment benefits through the Department of Labor. Available to Steuben County residents who lost jobs through no fault of their own. Benefits typically $400-600/week.

📞 1-888-209-8124 🌐 dol.ny.gov
Duration: Up to 26 weeks of regular benefits + extended benefits during downturns
Unemployment

NY Career Centers (One-Stop Career Center)

Job placement, resume assistance, career training, and employer connections through the American Job Center network serving Steuben County. Free workforce development services.

📞 1-888-209-8124 🌐 dol.ny.gov/career-centers
Jobs

ACCES-VR Vocational Rehabilitation

Free vocational rehabilitation and employment services for Steuben County residents with disabilities. Helps with job training, assistive technology, and employment placement.

📞 1-800-222-5627 🌐 access.nysed.gov
VR Services

Senior Services

Programs for adults 60+ in Steuben County

NY Office for the Aging (NYSOFA)

Connects Steuben County seniors to home-delivered meals (Meals on Wheels), transportation, in-home care, caregiver support, and community programs through the Area Agency on Aging.

📞 1-800-342-9871 🌐 aging.ny.gov
Seniors

Elderly Pharmaceutical Insurance Coverage (EPIC)

Free prescription drug insurance for Steuben County seniors age 65+ with limited income. Covers Medicare and non-Medicare eligible seniors with low drug costs.

📞 1-800-332-3742 🌐 health.ny.gov/epic
Income limit: Up to $35,000 annual income
Medicare

211 NY Senior Referral Line

Free, confidential referral service for Steuben County seniors. Connects you to Meals on Wheels, transportation, care management, and hundreds of local senior programs.

📞 Dial 211 🌐 211nys.org
Referral

Disability Services

SSI, SSDI, and disability support programs

ACCES-VR Disability Services

Comprehensive vocational rehabilitation, employment services, and assistive technology for Steuben County residents with disabilities. Free employment support and job training.

📞 1-800-222-5627 🌐 access.nysed.gov
VR Services

NY Commission for the Blind (NYSCB)

Services for Steuben County residents who are blind or visually impaired. Includes rehabilitation, employment services, assistive technology, and independent living support.

📞 1-866-871-3000 🌐 ocfs.ny.gov/nyscb
Blind Services

Social Security (SSI/SSDI)

Monthly income support for Steuben County residents with disabilities who cannot work. Supplemental Security Income (SSI) and Social Security Disability Insurance (SSDI) available.

📞 1-800-772-1213 🌐 ssa.gov
Disability

Child Care & Education

Subsidized childcare, Head Start, and early education

NY Child Care Assistance Program (CCAP)

Income-based childcare subsidies for Steuben County families. Helps pay for licensed childcare centers, family childcare, and school-age programs.

📞 1-800-342-3009 🌐 ocfs.ny.gov/childcare
Income limit: Up to 75% of state median income
Child Care

Head Start & Early Head Start

Free comprehensive early childhood education, health, and nutrition services for income-qualifying children ages 0–5 in Steuben County. Plus family support services.

📞 1-800-554-8624 🌐 eclkc.ohs.acf.hhs.gov
Head Start

Pre-K for All / Universal Pre-K (UPK)

Free universal pre-kindergarten for eligible Steuben County 4-year-olds through the state's Pre-K for All program. Provided at schools and licensed programs.

📞 1-800-342-3009 🌐 ocfs.ny.gov/programs/childcare
Pre-K

Community & Crisis Services

Emergency assistance, crisis support, and local organizations

211 NY Comprehensive Referral Service

Free, confidential helpline available 24/7 for Steuben County residents. Connects you to hundreds of local programs for any need including housing, food, utilities, health, employment, and crisis services.

📞 Dial 211 🌐 211nys.org
24/7 Crisis

NY Domestic Violence Hotline

Free, confidential support for Steuben County residents experiencing domestic violence. Available 24/7 for crisis intervention, safety planning, and referrals to shelters and services.

📞 1-800-942-6906 🌐 opdv.ny.gov
DV Support

Crisis Text Line

Free, confidential crisis support available 24/7 for Steuben County residents. Text HOME to 741741 to connect with a crisis counselor for any emotional or mental health emergency.

📱 Text HOME to 741741 🌐 crisistextline.org
Mental Health
