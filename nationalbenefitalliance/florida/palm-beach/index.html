Utility Assistance

Help paying electric, gas, and water bills in Palm Beach County

LIHEAP – Low Income Home Energy Assistance Program

Federal program providing energy bill assistance and crisis intervention for eligible low-income Palm Beach County residents. Covers electric, gas, and cooling costs.

📞 561-383-1900 🌐 Palm Beach County Community Services
Eligibility: Income at or below 150% of federal poverty level · Contact: (561) 355-4100
Energy

FPL / Duke Energy – Utility Customer Assistance

Florida utility companies offer bill payment plans, low-income discounts, and budget billing for qualifying customers in Palm Beach County.

📞 1-800-375-4357 🌐 fpl.com/assistance
Electric

211 Palm Beach — Utility Crisis Referral

Call 211 anytime for immediate referrals to emergency utility assistance programs in Palm Beach County, including after-hours crisis resources.

📞 Dial 211 🌐 www.211palmbeach.org
Referral

Housing & Rental Assistance

Rent help, eviction prevention, and housing stability programs

Palm Beach County Housing Authority — Emergency Rental Assistance

Emergency rental and mortgage assistance for Palm Beach County residents facing eviction or foreclosure due to financial hardship.

📞 (561) 684-2450 🌐 www.pbcha.org
Rental Aid

Section 8 Housing Choice Voucher Program

Federally-funded rental assistance vouchers for income-qualifying households in Palm Beach County. Apply through your local housing authority when waitlists open.

📞 (561) 684-2450 🌐 www.pbcha.org
Section 8

Florida Homeowner Assistance Fund (HAF)

Mortgage assistance for homeowners in Palm Beach County facing financial hardship. Covers mortgage payments, property taxes, insurance, and HOA fees.

📞 1-877-383-8899 🌐 floridahousing.org/HAF
Mortgage

Food Assistance

SNAP, food banks, WIC, and free meal programs

SNAP — Supplemental Nutrition Assistance Program (Food Stamps)

Apply for monthly food assistance through ACCESS Florida. Average Florida SNAP benefit: $210/month per person.

📞 1-866-762-2237 🌐 myflorida.com/accessflorida
Apply online or visit a DCF ACCESS Service Center in West Palm Beach · Processing: 30 days (7 days expedited)
SNAP

Feeding South Florida

Regional food bank serving Palm Beach County. Find the nearest food pantry, mobile distribution, or partner agency near you.

📞 (561) 253-7233 🌐 feedingsouthflorida.org
Food Bank

WIC — Women, Infants & Children Program

Monthly food benefits, nutrition counseling, and breastfeeding support for pregnant women, new mothers, and children under age 5 in Palm Beach County.

📞 1-800-447-2229 🌐 floridahealth.gov/wic
WIC

Healthcare & Medicaid

Free and low-cost healthcare, Medicaid enrollment, and clinics

Florida Medicaid — DCF Health Coverage

Free or low-cost health insurance for qualifying Palm Beach County residents including families, pregnant women, seniors, and people with disabilities.

📞 1-800-610-8301 🌐 myflorida.com/accessflorida
Medicaid

Florida Community Health Centers

Federally Qualified Health Centers (FQHCs) in Palm Beach County offer primary care, dental, and mental health services on a sliding-fee scale regardless of insurance status.

📞 1-800-548-4433 🌐 findahealthcenter.hrsa.gov
Health Clinic

Children's Health Insurance Program (CHIP / KidCare)

Low-cost health coverage for uninsured children in Palm Beach County who don't qualify for Medicaid. Covers doctor visits, prescriptions, dental, and vision.

📞 1-888-205-4636 🌐 floridakidcare.org
Children

Employment & Unemployment

Unemployment benefits, job training, and workforce programs

Florida DEO — Reemployment Assistance (Unemployment)

Apply for weekly unemployment benefits through Florida's Department of Economic Opportunity (CONNECT system). Available to Palm Beach County residents who lost jobs through no fault of their own.

📞 1-833-352-6362 🌐 connect.myflorida.com
Unemployment

CareerSource Florida — Workforce Development

Job placement, resume assistance, career training, and employer connections through the CareerSource center serving Palm Beach County.

📞 1-855-872-2299 🌐 careersourceflorida.com

Senior Services

Programs for adults 60+ in Palm Beach County

SHINE — Serving Health Insurance Needs of Elders

Free Medicare counseling and health insurance guidance for Palm Beach County seniors. Helps navigate Medicare Part A/B/D, Medicare Advantage, and supplements.

📞 1-800-363-0945 🌐 floridashine.org
Medicare

Elder Affairs — Palm Beach County Senior Services

Connects Palm Beach County seniors to home-delivered meals (Meals on Wheels), transportation, in-home care, caregiver support, and community programs through the Area Agency on Aging.
