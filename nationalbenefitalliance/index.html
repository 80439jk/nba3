FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I find benefit programs in my county? +

Enter your 5-digit ZIP code in the search box at the top of this page. We automatically identify your county and display all available assistance programs organized by category.

Do I need an access code? +

Basic county information is available without a code. The full detailed guide — including direct contacts, eligibility tips, and downloadable PDF — requires a free access code. Call 1-800-NBA-HELP to get yours.

Is this service really free? +

Yes. The National Benefit Alliance is a free public resource. We never charge any fees to access our guides, receive an access code, or download PDFs. If anyone asks you to pay, that is not us.

Can I get the guide emailed to me? +

Yes. On any county resource page, enter your email address and we will send a formatted PDF directly to your inbox. You can also download it directly from the page.

How often is the information updated? +

We review and update each county guide quarterly. Program phone numbers, addresses, and eligibility requirements are verified against official government and nonprofit sources.
